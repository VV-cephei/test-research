# Technical Analysis: MIL-PRF-22750G (with Amendment 1)

## Summary
MIL-PRF-22750G is a performance specification for a two-component, high-solids epoxy coating. It is lead and chromate-free, with a maximum VOC content of 340 g/l. This coating is primarily intended for interior use on aircraft, ground support equipment, and as a repair coating for Navy weapon systems.

## Technical Specifications

### Classification
- **Types**:
  - **Type II**: VOHAP-free formulation (replaces Type I).
  - **Type III**: Direct-to-metal (DTM), VOHAP-free, with enhanced corrosion resistance.
  - **Type IV**: Self-contained portable kits.
- **Classes**:
  - **Class H**: High solids, solvent base.
  - **Class W**: Water base.
- **Grades**:
  - **Grade A**: Weather resistant (Army/Navy).
  - **Grade B**: Interior use only, not weather resistant.

### Composition
- **Component A**: Epoxy resin and solvents.
- **Component B**: Polyamide and/or amine resin(s) and solvents (curing agent).
- **VOC Limit**: Maximum 340 g/l (2.8 lbs/gal).
- **Prohibitions**: No lead, cadmium, hexavalent chromium, or specific toxic HAPs (antimony, arsenic, etc.).

### Physical & Optical Properties
- **Gloss (60 degree)**:
  - Gloss: Min 90.
  - Semi-gloss: 15 - 30.
  - Lusterless/Flat: Max 5.
- **Drying Time**:
  - Set-to-touch: Max 4 hours.
  - Dry-hard: Max 8 hours.
- **Viscosity**: Max 50 seconds (#4 Ford Cup) after thinning.
- **Hiding Power**: Contrast ratio min 0.90 - 0.95.

### Performance & Resistance
- **Adhesion**: Scale 4B or better (Dry), 4A or better (Wet).
- **Fluid Resistance**:
  - Water: 168 hours immersion.
  - Hydrocarbon (JP8): 168 hours immersion.
  - Lubricating Oil (MIL-PRF-23699): 24 hours at 250°F.
  - Hydraulic Fluids (MIL-PRF-87257/83282): 150°F.
- **Corrosion (Type III only)**:
  - Salt Spray: 1,000 hours (ASTM B117).
  - Cyclic Corrosion: 40 cycles (GMW 14872).
- **Heat Resistance**: Delta E <= 2.0 after 60 mins at 250°F.
- **Solvent Resistance**: Must withstand 25 double rubs with MEK.
- **Strippability**: Min 90% removal using TT-R-2918 Type I remover.

## Critical Data Points
- **VOHAP-Free**: Types II and III must be free of volatile organic hazardous air pollutants.
- **Induction Time**: Admixed coating may require a 30-minute induction period before use.
- **Pot Life**: Admixed coating must be used within 4 hours.
- **Thickness**: Standard dry film thickness is 2.0 ± 0.2 mils.

## Application Requirements
- **Primer**: Typically used over MIL-DTL-53022, MIL-DTL-53030, MIL-PRF-23377, or MIL-PRF-85582 (except Type III DTM).
- **Surface Prep**: Steel (zinc phosphate) or Aluminum (MIL-DTL-5541 Type II).
- **Safety**: Requires SDS compliance and specific respiratory protection warnings (Surgeon General requirement).
