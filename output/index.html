Summary

MIL-C-83286B is a two-component, aliphatic isocyanate-based, oil-free urethane coating. Engineered for exterior aerospace applications, it provides exceptional durability and resistance to harsh environmental conditions and chemicals.

Summary

MIL-PRF-22750G is a high-solids, two-component epoxy coating. It is lead and chromate-free, designed for interior use on aircraft and ground support equipment. It offers excellent chemical resistance and is available in VOHAP-free formulations.
